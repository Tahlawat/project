About Us

Designed to improve the lives of people with speech and language disabilities, OneForAll is a robust and totally free communication platform. Our goal is to offer a full range of alternative and augmentative communication tools to enhance communication and promote a more inclusive society.

OneForAll can provide you with bespoke AAC solutions, text-to-speech capabilities, or symbol-based communication boards. Come along today to have the freedom to effectively communicate your needs, feelings, and opinions to those you love without having to pay for it.
